person class:
	info:
		name- noncode? identification
	stats:

		health- hit points
		mana- points that are used by magic spells
		agility- base chance of dodging
		vitality- determines max health
		magic essence- determines max mana
		luck- (player only) loot amount and rarity chance modifier
	technical:
		body- physics objects
		sprite-  the images that show it's physicality (currently just image and trans)
